# Nuclear Fission

This lesson is a placeholder for an introduction to nuclear fission.

## What This Lesson Will Cover

- What it means for a nucleus to split
- Why uranium and plutonium are important fission fuels
- Neutrons, chain reactions, and criticality
- How fission releases heat
- How reactors control fission safely

## Key Idea

Nuclear fission occurs when a heavy atomic nucleus splits into smaller nuclei. The reaction releases energy and additional neutrons, which can continue the chain reaction.
