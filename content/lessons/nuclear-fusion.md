# Nuclear Fusion

This lesson is a placeholder for an introduction to nuclear fusion.

## What This Lesson Will Cover

- What it means for light nuclei to combine
- Why fusion powers the Sun and other stars
- Temperature, pressure, and plasma
- Deuterium, tritium, and common fusion fuels
- Why controlled fusion is difficult on Earth

## Key Idea

Nuclear fusion occurs when light atomic nuclei combine into a heavier nucleus. Under the right conditions, fusion can release a large amount of energy.
