# Radioactive Decay

This lesson is a placeholder for an introduction to radioactive decay.

## What This Lesson Will Cover

- Why some nuclei are unstable
- Alpha, beta, and gamma decay
- Half-life and decay chains
- How radioactive decay releases energy
- Examples from nature, medicine, and nuclear technology

## Key Idea

Radioactive decay happens when an unstable atomic nucleus changes into a more stable form. In the process, it can emit particles, energy, or both.
