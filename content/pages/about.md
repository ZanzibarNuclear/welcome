# About Atomic Ambitions

If you want to thrive in life, it helps to understand how things work. Atoms are the building blocks of everything. Having solid mental models of how atoms are put together and how they behave gives us a firm basis for understanding the world.

Atomic Ambitions is all about empowerment.

With knowledge comes courage and confidence. Atomic Ambitions will help you build that knowledge. Read all about atoms and other basics. Find out about atomic reactions that generate heat. Marvel at the techonology we have invented for turning atomic energy into electricity, clean water, and medical treatments.
