# Nuclear Reactors

Nuclear reactors are machines for managing chain reactions. They use fuel, coolant, control systems, and shielding to turn nuclear energy into heat, research neutrons, medical isotopes, propulsion, or electricity.

Get the nitty gritty details on each reactor type: what the design is trying to accomplish, how it moves heat, what safety ideas it relies on, and where it has been used.
