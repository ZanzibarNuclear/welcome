# Molten Salt Reactor

A molten salt reactor, or MSR, uses liquid salt to move heat out of the reactor core. Some designs keep fuel in solid form, while others dissolve the fuel directly into the circulating salt.

## Why It Matters

Molten salt systems can operate at high temperatures and low pressure, which makes them interesting for efficient electricity generation, industrial heat, and advanced fuel cycles.
