# CANDU Reactor

CANDU stands for Canada Deuterium Uranium. It is a heavy-water reactor design that uses deuterium oxide as moderator and coolant, allowing the reactor to run on natural uranium fuel.

## Why It Matters

CANDU reactors are notable for their fuel flexibility and online refueling. Instead of shutting down the whole reactor to replace fuel, CANDU designs can refuel individual pressure tubes while operating.
