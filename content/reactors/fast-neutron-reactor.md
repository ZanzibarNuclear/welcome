# Fast Neutron Reactor

A fast neutron reactor avoids a moderator so neutrons remain at higher energies. This changes the reactor physics and can support different fuel strategies than thermal reactors.

## Why It Matters

Fast reactors are often discussed for fuel breeding, waste reduction, and more complete use of uranium resources. They also bring distinct engineering challenges around coolant choice and materials.
